Samsung TV Problems We Fix Every Day

We handle every type of Samsung TV issue, from simple software glitches to complex hardware failures. Here are the most common Samsung TV problems our customers bring to us:

💡

Backlight Failure

Samsung TV has sound but the screen is dark or very dim? This is usually a backlight strip failure - one of the most common Samsung TV issues. We replace individual LED strips instead of the entire panel, saving you thousands.

📷

Panel & Display Issues

Vertical lines, horizontal bands, dead pixels, colour patches, or a cracked screen. We repair or replace Samsung TV panels with matched replacements for a perfect picture.

⚡

Power Supply Board

Samsung TV not turning on? Red light blinking? Clicks but won't start? Power board issues are very common in Samsung TVs, especially after voltage fluctuations. We repair or replace the board same-day.

💻

Motherboard & T-Con

No picture, distorted display, or HDMI ports not working? The main board or T-Con board may need repair. We do component-level repair on Samsung motherboards.

🔌

Smart TV / Tizen Issues

Samsung Smart TV apps not loading? WiFi not connecting? Tizen OS frozen or slow? We fix all Samsung Smart Hub and software issues including firmware updates.

🎧

Sound Problems

No audio, crackling sound, sound delay, or speakers buzzing? We repair Samsung TV internal speakers and audio processing circuits for clear, balanced sound.
