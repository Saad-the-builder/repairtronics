Sony TV Blink Codes - We Decode Them All

Sony TVs have a unique diagnostic feature: when something fails, the standby LED blinks a specific number of times to indicate the problem. A 2-blink code means something different from a 6-blink code. Many repair shops do not know how to read these codes and end up replacing parts by trial and error. At Repair Tronics, our Ex-Sony Engineer Saad knows every Sony blink code and what it means - so we diagnose accurately on the first attempt and only replace what actually needs fixing. This saves you time and money.
