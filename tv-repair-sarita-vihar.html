Backlight Repair

The most common issue - your TV screen goes dark but you can still hear sound. We replace failed backlight strips on all brands, restoring your TV's picture quality at a fraction of replacement cost.

⚡

Power Board Issues

TV not turning on after a power cut? Delhi's voltage surges can damage power supply boards. We repair or replace power boards to get your TV running safely again.

📷

Display & Panel Problems

Lines on the screen, colour distortion, flickering, or dead zones? We diagnose whether it is a panel, T-Con board, or cable issue and fix it affordably.

Smart TV Software Fixes

Android TV, WebOS, or Tizen OS problems - apps crashing, WiFi not connecting, TV frozen on logo screen. We handle all smart TV software issues for every platform.

Audio Problems

No sound, one speaker not working, or buzzing noise? We repair internal speakers and audio circuits. Also fix soundbar and HDMI ARC connection issues.

Port & Connectivity Repair
