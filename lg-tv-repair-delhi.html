Common LG TV Problems We Solve

LG TVs are generally reliable, but certain issues come up more frequently than others. Here are the LG TV problems we see and fix most often:

💻

WebOS Issues

LG Smart TV stuck on loading screen? WebOS apps crashing or not installing? Content Store not working? We fix all WebOS software issues including factory resets and firmware recovery.

🎮

Magic Remote Problems

LG Magic Remote not pairing, cursor not showing, or buttons not responding? We diagnose whether it is a remote issue or an IR receiver problem on the TV and fix both.

💡

Backlight Failure

LG TV screen is dark but sound works? This is the most common LG hardware issue - the LED backlight strips fail. We replace them with quality strips that last, at a fraction of panel replacement cost.

📷

Panel & Display Defects

Vertical coloured lines, white spots, dead zones, or image retention on your LG TV? We assess whether a panel repair or replacement is needed and give you honest advice on the best path forward.

⚡

Power Board Failure

LG TV not turning on? Standby light blinking? Power cycling randomly? LG TVs are known for power supply board issues after 3-5 years. We repair or replace power boards same-day.

🔌

WiFi & Connectivity

LG TV not connecting to WiFi? Bluetooth not working? HDMI ports showing no signal? We fix all connectivity issues on LG Smart TVs including WiFi module replacement.

LG TV Backlight Repair - Our Specialty

Backlight failure is the single most common issue we see in LG TVs, especially models from 2015 to 2021. Many customers are told they need a full panel replacement costing Rs 15,000-30,000. In most cases, the actual fix is replacing a few LED backlight strips - which costs a fraction of that. At Repair Tronics, we always diagnose the root cause first and give you the most cost-effective solution. If we can save you money with a backlight strip replacement instead of a full panel swap, we will.
